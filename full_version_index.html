Full Version Tools :

Tool features are explained in detail. Click the open button to open the tool.

🛒 Grocery List :

Build your grocery list before you go shopping. Add items by typing the item name into the input box and pressing the add button. Check items off as you shop and lock your selections with the lock button.

📖 Cookbook :

Hundreds of chef-inspired recipes sorted and alphabetized into categories. Each recipe card opens in its own popup window so you can keep your place in the menu. Add recipes to your favorites list or send them straight to the meal planner calendar.

Open Tool

🔎 Recipe Finder :

Search the entire cookbook by recipe name or ingredients. The recipe finder scans every category and highlights every match so you can find exactly what you're looking for.

Open Tool

♡ Favorites List :

Your personal collection of saved recipes all in one place. Save any recipe from the cookbook to your favorites list and access them any time.

Open Tool

📅 Monthly Planner :

Plan a different meal every day of the month by choosing recipes from the cookbook or your favorites list. You can also create your own recipe cards and save them to favorites for use in the planner.
