Grocery List : Free !

The grocery checklist helps you keep track of the items you shop for. With the free version, you can add items by typing a product name and pressing the plus button. Meal Planner Subscribers get access to the preloaded grocery list plus A.I. cookbook suggestions.

When you load the grocery checklist with items that look like a recipe from our cookbook, A.I. will offer to show you a suggested recipe card that has the preparation instructions. Then you can prepare meals you didnt know how to.
