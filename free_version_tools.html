Free Meal Planner Tools :

Grocery Checklist :

Basic Features :

This tools purpose is to help you maintain a neat and orderly way to see what you want to shop for at the grocery store. You can add products to the checklist. Each product you add will have an empty checkbox beside it.

Before you go to the store, add items to the checklist. As you shop, put a check in the box beside the items you put in your cart. This helps you keep track of items as you shop for them.

If you change your mind, just uncheck the box or press the del button to remove the item from the list.

Full Version Features :

Microphone Button :

Some people are impaired by disabilities and the microphone allows disabled people to add items by speaking to the microphone.

Lock Button :

Lock button gives the ability to lock the checklist in place so no accidental changes are made.

A.I. Suggestions :

A.I. will offer to show recipes from the cookbook based on what you add to the grocery checklist.

Grocery List Menu :

Preloaded Grocery list menu that allows you to add items to the checklist by clicking an add button.

All available updates :

Updates are free of charge after initial purchase. Updates will include the ability of A.I. to keep track of what you buy and offer suggested coupons from local retailers. The app pays for itself when you save money with A.I. coupon suggestions.
